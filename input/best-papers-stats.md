**Key patterns observed:**
1. **Method is the largest section** (27–44% of body), reflecting the expectation that best papers make a clear technical contribution.
2. **Experiments+Results together are 30–40%** of body, with detailed tables, ablations, and sensitivity analysis.
3. **Introduction is 6–10%** of body (~550–925 words), establishing motivation and contributions.
4. **Related Work is 3–12%** — varies by paper; some keep it compact, others expand to position novelty.
5. **Conclusion is brief** (2–4% of body), summarizing without introducing new content.
6. **All papers include explicit limitations or scope discussion**, a hallmark of mature scholarship.
